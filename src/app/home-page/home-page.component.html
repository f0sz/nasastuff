Astronomy Picture of the Day

Discover the cosmos! Each day a different image or photograph of our fascinating universe is featured, along with a brief explanation written by a professional astronomer.

Earth Polychromatic Imaging Camera

The EPIC page provides information on the daily imagery collected by DSCOVR's Earth Polychromatic Imaging Camera (EPIC) instrument. Uniquely positioned at the Earth-Sun Lagrange point, EPIC provides full disc imagery of the Earth and captures unique perspectives of certain astronomical events.

Mars Rovers

This page is designed to browse images collected by NASA's Curiosity, Opportunity, and Spirit rovers on Mars. Each rover has it's own set of cameras with unique perspectives.
